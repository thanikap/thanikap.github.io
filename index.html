About Me

Featured Research

Machine Learning Prediction of Successful Rocket Launches at NASA Kennedy Using Lightning Data

Cambridge Center for International Research Dr. Hannah Rana, Mentor Summer 2025

Overview: This research addresses the critical challenge of weather-related launch delays by developing an ML model that predicts launch success using historical lightning data from NASA's KSC.

Key Contributions & Findings

  • Developed an SVM model with SMOTE oversampling to correct for highly imbalanced launch data.
  • Identified a key 915 V/m electric field threshold as a reliable predictor for go/no-go launch decisions.
  • Demonstrated that the model offers a data-driven, quantitative alternative to purely subjective safety assessments.
